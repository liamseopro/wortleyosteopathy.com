About the practice

Calm, considered care in the heart of Wortley Village.

Wortley Osteopathy is a small, independent manual osteopathy practice on Wortley Road in London, Ontario. Treatments are unhurried, one-on-one, and built around what your body is telling us, not a one-size-fits-all protocol.

Whether you are recovering from injury, managing chronic pain, or simply feeling “off,” osteopathic treatment works with your body’s own ability to heal: gentle manual techniques that release tension, restore mobility, and calm the nervous system.

What osteopathy can help with

Every treatment is tailored to you. These are the concerns patients most often come in with, though you don’t need a “reason” beyond wanting to feel better.

Back & Neck Pain

Acute or chronic spinal tension, postural strain, and disc-related discomfort addressed through gentle joint and soft-tissue work.

Headaches & Migraines

Cranial and cervical techniques that calm the nervous system and reduce the frequency and intensity of tension headaches.

Joint Mobility

Stiff shoulders, hips, knees, and ankles restored through articulation, fluid movement, and targeted manual release.

Digestive Health

Visceral manipulation supports healthy organ rhythm and can ease bloating, reflux, and stress-driven digestive tension.

Posture & Alignment

For desk workers, drivers, and anyone held in one position too long: rebalance the structure, ease the strain.

Stress & Nervous System

Subtle craniosacral and fascial work that down-shifts the fight-or-flight response and lets the body actually rest.
